Expert Setup Tips

Start with High-Traffic Areas

Focus on living room and kitchen first - these are where most accidents happen.

Think Like Your Pet

Get down to their eye level and look for potential hazards from their perspective.

Create Designated Zones

Establish clear eating, sleeping, and play areas to reduce confusion and accidents.

Common Setup Mistakes

Forgetting Vertical Spaces

Cats and birds need vertical territory - don't just pet-proof at ground level.

Overlooking Electrical Cords

Hidden cords behind furniture are still accessible - secure all electrical hazards.

Not Testing Pet Access

Walk through your setup with your pet to identify missed hazards or access issues.
